🎯 The Problem We Solve

Legal Unawareness

Most Indian citizens don't know their basic legal rights. A lawyer consultation costs ₹2,000–₹10,000 per hour — unaffordable for many.

✅

Our Solution

LexAI retrieves the exact relevant sections from Indian law and explains them in plain, simple language — instantly and for free.

🧠 How It Works

1

You describe your problem

Type your legal problem in plain English. No legal jargon needed.

2

ML Classifier identifies the domain

A trained Random Forest model classifies your query into the correct legal domain — Criminal, Civil, Tax, Labour, or Consumer Law.

3

RAG retrieves relevant law sections

Our vector database searches 12,000+ chunks from official Indian law documents and retrieves the most relevant sections.

4

AI generates a clear explanation

An LLM synthesizes the retrieved legal text into simple, actionable advice tailored to your specific situation.
